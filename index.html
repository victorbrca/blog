Gnome Touchpad Settings Missing in Arch Xps 13

23 Sep, 2017 Linux Arch Hardware

I had an issue where the Gnome extension ‘Touchpad Indicator’ stopped working on my xps 13 (Arch). After looking a bit further, it seems that the Gnome Touchpad settings had also stopped working. All I could see was the mouse settings, and the touchpad section was completelly gone. Solution: With Gnome 3.20, xf86-input-synaptics is not longer supported, and you should use xf86-input-libinput instead. You can check what is installed on your Arch system with pacman -Q | grep input.
